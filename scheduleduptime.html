Welcome to scheduleduptime.com. We strive to achieve 100% downtime. Unfortunately, due to the realities of new users and occasional updated content, some amount of uptime is unavoidable.

However, in these cases we are committed to providing our users with the information necessary to plan around these inages.

The next scheduled uptime is available by performing a DNS query for TXT records on the scheduleduptime.com domain. This web page is available over HTTP at the times indicated. The VM running this site shuts down during all other times.

If you're interested in our content but not able to access it during scheduled uptime, Here is a node.js script that will determine the next uptime window and retrieve the HTML content at the appropriate time, saving it to scheduleduptime.html to be read at your leisure.
